Writing Center Tutor

Illinois Institute of Technology: Fall 2009

As a writing center tutor at IIT, I adopted a directive approach in assisting students in their writing projects. I assisted students primarily with projects relating to scientific reports and papers, art and architectural history, and professional development documents such as résumés, CV's, and applications for graduate study.

Writing Consultant

DePaul University: 2006-2008

My approach to tutoring writing at DePaul University rested on a balance between directive and non-directive strategies. For instance, I advocated for and employed much more directive strategies for English as a Target Language (ETL) students as a result of my experiences collaborating with the English Language Academy. As a consultant, I worked with hundreds of students from various disciplines.

Freelance Writing Consultant

2008-Present

In my work as a freelance writing consultant I have mainly worked with graduate students on completing degree requirement writings such as dissertation proposals, and in one case, an entire dissertation. I also have experience working with academic professionals to improve their professional and business writing skills.
